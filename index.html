Bio

After a master's degree in applied mathematics at the La Rochelle University. I am currently a PhD student in computer science at IRISA on the Intuidoc team. I work on the KIHT project which consists in designing and developing the deep learning AI that reconstructs online handwriting trajectories from the DigiPen’s kinematic sensors (accelerometers, gyroscope, magnetometer, force sensors). This project is in partnership with Stabilo, KIT and Learn&Go.
